About the Blogger

Graduating with a Media and Information degree can be exciting and daunting. As a student, I am challenged to decide which career path to take. In this blog, I will explore my journey as I make this important decision. From researching different job opportunities to learning how to use new technologies, I will share my experiences and insights as I navigate my way through the ever-changing world of media.

This blog will discuss how media roles have been affected by this change and the challenges of transitioning to new positions. It will also explore some of the significant design careers available in this field and how they are helping shape its future.
